Description

Toni Gonzaga's "We Belong" is a love song where the singer expresses her certainty that she and her partner are destined to be together, finding solace and permanence in their relationship.

The lyrics speak of a beginning from the start, with the partner as a source of shelter and a promise of an everlasting future together, as highlighted in Apple Music's and Shazam's descriptions and lyrics.
